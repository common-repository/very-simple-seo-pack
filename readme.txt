A Very Simple SEO Pack plugin that enables you to add SEO Meta and Open Graph Meta content on all your posts and/or pages.

== Description ==

The Very Simple SEO Pack does exactly what it says on the tin – adds seo and open graph meta content to all of your posts and pages, simply.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'very-simple-seo-pack' folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. After activate meta boxes will be publish under the content editor on every post/page.
